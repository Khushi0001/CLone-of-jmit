Instructions for filling Online Application Form

  1. If you pay registration fee by Demand Draft

    1. Demand draft must be ready before filling the online application since Demand Draft details are to be entered during filling of Application Form. Demand Draft of Rs. 500/- (Rs. Five Hundred only) must be drawn in favor of "Seth Jai Parkash Mukand Lal Institute of Engineering & Technology, Radaur" payable at Radaur.
    2. After successful submission of Application Form, take print out of the application form and send the signed application form after pasting your recent photograph along-with the Demand Draft to the address "Seth Jai Parkash Mukand Lal Institute of Engineering and Technology"
    3. On the reverse side of the Demand Draft please mention your Name, Application No. and Qualifying Exam Rank if any.

    For any query please contact at 8295905206 , 8295905227.
