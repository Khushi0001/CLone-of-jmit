Director's Message
Dear Students & Worthy Parents
“Earn the Skills: Be the Change”

This is an ecstatic moment for me to reach out to you in sharing our tireless efforts in grooming future leaders, inspiring each student in shaping his/her tomorrow while taking you to the threshold of the new beginning of your professional career. I am overwhelmed to commune with you as Director of Seth Jai Parkash Mukand Lal Institute of Engineering & Technology (JMIT), Radaur, Haryana and welcome you to be in a professional environment, to aspire for a brighter tomorrow, for we aim to groom quality human resources for the Nation.
As I see, our students are climbing up their career ladder successfully as each year passes by. I wholeheartedly thank our team of faculty members for their incessant efforts in grooming each student and the Management Team with Dr. Ramesh Kumar, General Secretary and other learned colleagues for their excellent and applaudable support. We are blessed by the vision and valuable guidance of our Chairman, Sh. Ashok Kumar Ji.

We have created the following Centres of Excellence in our Institute to provide skill based education, hands on training and to meet the requirement of the Industrial Evolution 4.0 in association with the corporate beyond the curriculum:-

– Industrial Instrumentation & Automation
– Texas Instruments
– Robotics
– Daikin Air Conditioning
– Solar Centre of Excellence
– Information & Communication Lab (ICT)

The Institute has 50 clubs and separate dedicated Personality Development Department to transform the students as per corporate need. Cultural, sports & social activities are conducted regularly for the overall development of the students because the objective of the Institute is to impart moral based education alongwith technical education which helps them in becoming a good human being and a responsible citizen.

Our students are actively taking part in technical and cultural activities and brought laurels to the Institute by securing university positions, prizes at National & International levels like Smart India Hackathon etc. Our students are placed in well reputed companies like TCS, Microsoft, Cognizant, Escorts Ltd., MCDonald, Just Dial, Bajaj Finance etc. which has national & international recognition. Alongwith successful Technocrats, Managers, Entrepreneurs, many of our students are IAS, IES, IRS & IFS also.

With this conviction we march ahead, open new vistas for learning and empowering the youth with knowledge, innovation and better understanding.
On this new dawn of ever expansive infinite domain of knowledge we are excited to welcome you!
Come, Be a Mukandian, Earn the Skills and Be the Change!

Director
Dr. S.K. Garg
